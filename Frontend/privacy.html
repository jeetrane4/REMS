Privacy Policy

REMS (Real Estate Management System) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email, and mobile number during registration
  • Property listing data submitted by sellers/agents
  • Booking and transaction-related information
  • Basic usage analytics for improving system performance

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve REMS services:

  • Property listing, searching, and recommendations
  • Managing bookings and user interactions
  • Processing transactions (simulated/demo)
  • Enhancing platform security and performance

Cookies & Storage

REMS uses browser storage (localStorage) and cookies for:

  • Authentication (JWT token storage)
  • User preferences (theme, settings)

Data Security

We implement authentication, authorization, and database-level protections to ensure your data is handled securely.

Limitations

This platform is developed as an academic project. Some features like payments and notifications are simulated.
