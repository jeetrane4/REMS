Privacy Policy

REMS (Real Estate Management System) respects the privacy of its users. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email during account registration
  • Property listing information submitted by sellers
  • Booking requests made by buyers
  • Basic usage analytics for system improvement

How We Use Your Data

The collected information is used to provide platform services including:

  • Property listing and discovery
  • Managing property bookings
  • Tracking transactions
  • Improving platform functionality

Cookies

REMS may use cookies or browser storage to maintain session data such as authentication tokens and user preferences.

Data Security

We implement authentication and access controls to ensure that user data is securely handled within the platform.

Academic Project Notice

This platform is developed as an academic project. Some features such as payments are simulated and do not involve real financial transactions.
